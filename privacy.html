Privacy Policy

Last updated: 14 March 2026

Overview

Baby Data is a baby tracking app that respects your privacy. We do not collect, store, or transmit any of your data.

Data Storage

All data you enter (events, regimes, profile, backups) is stored locally on your device using your browser's localStorage. Your data never leaves your device.

No Data Collection

We do not collect any personal information, usage analytics, crash reports, or telemetry of any kind.

No Third-Party Services

Baby Data does not use any third-party services, APIs, advertising networks, or tracking tools.

No Account Required

Baby Data does not require you to create an account or sign in. There are no servers involved.

Data Export

You can export your data at any time using the Export feature in the app menu. The exported file is saved directly to your device.

Children's Privacy

While Baby Data is used to track information about babies, no data about your child is collected or transmitted. All information remains on your device under your control.

Changes to This Policy

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted on this page with a new date.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please open an issue on our GitHub repository.
